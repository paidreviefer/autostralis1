For Manufacturers

“Your Installation‑Ready Infrastructure Partner” — we deliver bays on time and to spec, reducing after‑sales issues and protecting brand reputation.

1) Civil Planning & Pit Construction

  • Site‑ready bay drawings aligned with OEM footprints
  • Pit excavation & construction to specified dimensions
  • Slope & drainage level design for optimal water flow
  • Foundation setting for gantries, conveyors, and machinery

2) MEP & Utilities Setup

  • Plumbing: supply lines, high‑pressure feed, return loops
  • Oil traps & water recycling units for eco‑compliance

  • Electrical paneling & automation cabling for safety/control
  • BIS safety compliance checks

3) Equipment Integration

  • Installation & alignment: wash, steam, vacuum, extraction, compressed air
  • Controls & automation modules connection
  • Testing & commissioning with water and power load simulations

4) Interiors & Branding

  • Industrial‑grade, chemical‑resistant flooring
  • Washable wall treatments and practical finishes
  • Lighting design for work efficiency and visual appeal
  • Brand asset implementation: signage, decals, customer displays

5) Handover & Documentation

  • As‑built drawings & O&M manuals
  • Basic operational training for client staff

6) Post‑Completion Support

  • On‑call technical assistance
  • Periodic site inspections (preventive maintenance)
  • Support for upgrades and future expansions
