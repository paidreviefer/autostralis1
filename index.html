About Us

Autostralis is a premium automotive infrastructure partner, delivering turnkey car wash and detailing facilities from bare space to business‑ready launch. We serve car wash & detailing equipment manufacturers and investors/entrepreneurs with end‑to‑end solutions — civil works, MEP, equipment integration, interiors, branding, and operational handover.

Backed by the Mangalathu Group’s decade‑plus expertise in civil contracting, interiors, and project execution, Autostralis has successfully delivered 200+ automotive facilities across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka.
